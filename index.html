Empty Your Cup Sharing

As we get older, we fill up our cups with our past experiences and knowledge. We are out of patience and space so often, we meet the opinions of others with hostility. We meet life with bitterness. In this retreat we offer safe space to share your burdens.

The Silent Retreat

The silent retreat means taking a vow of silence for the duration of the retreat. Besides no verbal communication, the silence extends to no reading, journaling, or the usage of phones/tablets. It's an invitation for a digital detox and noise purification.

Dynamic meditation

Dynamic meditation is a form of meditation in which physical actions are involved. The process allows meditation to emerge through rapid breathing, voicing emotions and active movement. 1 session lasts a full hour and consists of 5 stages.
